ATMOUSEPHERES 2.5 — AROUND THE PARKS FOUNDATION

UPLOAD EVERY FILE IN THIS FOLDER TO THE ROOT OF TESLAPAGES.

WHAT IS NEW
- Around the Parks now opens its own full-screen Magic Kingdom page.
- Six destination buttons: Main Street, Adventureland, Frontierland,
  Liberty Square, Fantasyland and Tomorrowland.
- Working selection, previous/next, volume, Back and Home controls.
- Responsive layout for Tesla, MacBook and phone screens.
- Music feed slots are built into around-parks.html using data-feed attributes.

IMPORTANT
- This is the experience and player foundation.
- The six data-feed values are intentionally blank until each legal,
  reliable audio source is selected and tested.
- Do not paste ordinary YouTube page links into data-feed. It requires a
  direct browser-playable audio stream URL.
